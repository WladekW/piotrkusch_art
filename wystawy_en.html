PIOTR KUSCH born in 1965 in Starogard Gdański, Poland. He graduated from the Laski Secondary School for the Blind. Sculpting in wood, stone and clay. He’s been having individual as well as group exhibitions in Poland and other European countries such as Great Britain, Germany, France, Hungary and Norway. Since 2024 he is a member of the Artists Association KERAMOS (Stowarzyszenie Artystów Ceramików KERAMOS).

Education:

  • 1982 – 1985 metal-plastic workshop at Jacek Rochacki’s studio
  • 1987 – 1995 working together at Barbara Zbrożyna’s sculpture studio
  • 2000 – Participant in the ceramic-course organized by Albert and Poli Krystyniak at the Ethnographic Museum in Węgorzewo
